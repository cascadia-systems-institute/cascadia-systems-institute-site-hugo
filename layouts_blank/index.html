Executive Summary

The Cascadia Systems Institute (CSI) believes systems science and systems thinking are the most important skills humans need to solve the problems of the future. Our mission is to educate the general public and institutional leaders in these skills and provide resources for communities and organizations to shift their systems toward a model based on sustainability.
